Controls

Hover with the mouse

Hover over squares in the graph to view their task information. If using the Deadline, Subject, or Department field, hover over the color scale to see the corresponding values.

Click with the mouse

Click a square in the graph to bring up a form to change a certain field of the corresponding task. Double-click anywhere on the graph to create a new square and submit the form that appears to add it. If your sheet has both Deadline, Subject, or Department clicking the color scale to change scales.

Drag with the mouse

Click, hold, and drag a square anywhere on the graph to change its position. Click, hold, and drag a task in the Completed or Unassigned list to move it from one to the other or onto the graph.

Press the Enter or Delete button

Press the Enter button to display each square's id and a table of the corresponding task names/descriptions. When editing a square, the Delete button can be pressed to delete the square and all of its information.

Press Ctrl + C or Ctrl + U

When editing a square, press Ctrl + C to move the square's information to the Completed section and remove the square from the graph; press Ctrl + U to move the square's information to the Unassigned section and remove the square from the graph.
