Controls

Hover with the mouse

Hover over squares in the graph to view their task information. If using the deadline field, hover over the color scale to see the corresponding values.

Click with the mouse

Click a square in the graph to bring up a form to change a certain field of the corresponding task. Double-click anywhere on the graph to create a new square and submit the form that appears to add it.

Drag with the mouse

Click, hold, and drag a square anywhere on the graph to change its position.

Press the Enter or Control button

Press the Enter button to display each square's id and a table of the corresponding task names/descriptions. When editing a square, the control button can be pressed to delete the square and all of its information.
